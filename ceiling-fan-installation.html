Fan Services We Offer in Las Vegas

Installing a Fan Where No Wiring Exists

You want a fan — but there's no wiring on the ceiling. We fix that.

A new circuit runs from your panel. A fan-rated box goes in. A wall switch gets added. Then the unit gets hung. Every ceiling fan installation Las Vegas job we take is done to code. No shortcuts.

Replacing an Old Fan or Light Fixture

Swapping an old unit or light is quick when the wiring is already there. We disconnect it, check the box and wiring, and hang the new fan.

If the box isn't fan-rated — common in older homes here — we swap it first. A unit on the wrong box is a safety hazard. We never skip that step.

Outdoor & Patio Fan Service Las Vegas

Electrical Requirements

Wiring & Electrical Requirements for Fans

Fan-Rated Electrical Box Requirement

Not every ceiling box can hold a unit. These are heavy and they vibrate. A standard light box can fail under that load — and drop the fan.

A proper fan-rated box goes in on every ceiling fan installation Las Vegas job. It's a code requirement and a basic safety rule.

Running New Wiring for a Fan Circuit

When there's no existing wiring, we run a new circuit. We route the wire neatly through the wall and ceiling. A wall switch gets added — or a dual switch for fan speed and light control on separate controls.

We also install smart switches that work with Alexa and Google Home.

Fan Options

Fan Types & Features We Handle in Las Vegas

Smart & Remote-Controlled Fans

Most new units come with a remote. Some pair with smart home systems. We install them with remotes, pull chains, wall controls, and smart switches — whatever your setup needs.

Fans with Built-In LED Light Kits

Most units we install come with LED light kits. These use far less power than older bulb-style models. We connect the light kit and confirm both the fan and light work on the right controls.

High-Ceiling & Hugger Fan Options

Standard units need at least 7 feet of clearance below the blades. Homes with high or vaulted ceilings need a longer downrod to bring the fan to the right height.

Low-ceiling rooms need a flush-mount unit — no downrod. Ceiling height gets checked on every ceiling fan installation Las Vegas job. Right hardware for the room.
