Safety

Electrical Safety for Las Vegas Homes

The heat here is rough on wiring. Years of desert heat break down wire insulation faster than in cooler places. DIY work done without permits causes problems too. Bad connections. Small circuits. Open boxes inside walls.

If your house is over 20 years old with no expert check, start there.

Warning Signs You Need Electrical Work

Watch for these. Breakers tripping on the same circuit. Lights dimming when appliances kick on. Outlets that quit for no reason. A burning smell near an outlet — stop using that circuit and call right away. No GFCI in the bathroom, kitchen, or garage. A panel that runs warm or buzzes.

A residential electrician Las Vegas homeowners trust will be straight with you. They tell you what needs fixing. They tell you what's fine.
