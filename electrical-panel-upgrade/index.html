Why It Matters

Why Las Vegas Homes Need Electrical Panel Upgrades

The average home here runs AC for months. Add EV chargers, smart home gear, and home offices and the load adds up fast. An old 100-amp panel wasn't built for any of that.

Signs Your Electrical Panel Is Outdated or Failing

Watch for these in your home. Breakers that trip often on the same circuit — a load or wiring issue. A breaker box that feels warm or makes buzzing sounds — needs attention now. Lights that dim when appliances kick on — the system is struggling. No room left to add new circuits — you've hit the limit.

If your home still has a fuse box, that's the first thing to fix.

Breaker Panels That Don't Meet Modern Load Demands

Federal Pacific and Zinsco boxes from the 1970s and 1980s have a known failure problem. Their breakers don't always trip when they should. That's a fire risk. If you have one, replacement isn't optional — it's urgent.

A modern 200-amp breaker box handles today's load. And gives you room to grow.

100-Amp to 200-Amp Service Upgrade

This is our most common job. Older homes in Las Vegas were built with 100-amp service. That's not enough now. AC, EV chargers, and modern appliances need more. A 200-amp upgrade fixes the capacity problem and opens up room for new circuits.

200-Amp to 400-Amp Panel Upgrade for Large Homes

Larger homes with pools, multiple AC units, or EV chargers sometimes need 400-amp service. We assess the load, coordinate with NV Energy, and install the larger service when needed.

Fuse Box to Breaker Panel Conversion

Still on a fuse box? That needs to change. Fuse boxes can't be expanded, don't protect circuits the way breakers do, and fail code on most home sales. We convert fuse boxes to modern breaker panels throughout the valley.
