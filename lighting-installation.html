Indoor Lighting Installation Services

Indoor lighting serves multiple purposes from task lighting that helps you work safely to ambient lighting that creates comfortable atmospheres. Our comprehensive indoor lighting services cover every room in your home.

Kitchen Lighting Solutions

Kitchens require layered lighting that combines ambient, task, and accent lighting. We design and install complete kitchen lighting systems that illuminate countertops, eliminate shadows, and create inviting spaces for cooking and gathering.

Recessed lights provide general illumination throughout kitchens. We space recessed lights properly to provide even coverage without dark spots. Under-cabinet LED lighting illuminates countertops for food preparation and cooking. These lights eliminate shadows that overhead lighting creates, making kitchen work safer and easier.

Pendant lights over kitchen islands add style while providing focused task lighting. We install pendants at appropriate heights that provide good illumination without blocking sight lines across the kitchen. Dimmer switches allow you to adjust lighting levels from bright task lighting to softer ambient lighting for dining.

Bathroom Lighting Installation

Bathroom lighting must provide adequate illumination for grooming tasks while creating pleasant atmospheres. We install vanity lights at proper heights and distances from mirrors to minimize shadows on faces. This positioning provides flattering light for applying makeup, shaving, and other grooming activities.

All bathroom lighting installations include fixtures rated for damp locations as required by building codes. We ensure proper GFCI protection for all bathroom electrical work. Bathrooms with showers or tubs need fixtures rated for wet locations in areas where water spray is likely. We ensure all bathroom lighting meets safety requirements for moisture exposure.

Recessed lights or ceiling fixtures provide general bathroom illumination. We recommend fixtures with appropriate light output for bathroom sizes. LED fixtures provide excellent light quality while using minimal electricity, important for lights that often run for extended periods.

Living Room and Bedroom Lighting

Living rooms and bedrooms need flexible lighting that adapts to different activities and times of day. We install ceiling fixtures, recessed lights, and wall sconces that provide ambient lighting. Dimmer switches allow you to adjust light levels from bright for reading and activities to soft for relaxing and watching television.

Ceiling fans with integrated lights provide both air circulation and illumination in bedrooms. We install fan-light combinations that meet your needs for comfort and lighting. Separate controls for fan and light functions provide maximum flexibility.

Recessed lighting creates clean, modern looks in living areas. We design recessed lighting layouts that provide even illumination throughout rooms. Proper spacing prevents dark spots and overlighting while using the minimum number of fixtures needed for adequate coverage.

Dining Room Chandeliers

Dining room chandeliers serve as focal points while providing ambient lighting for meals and gatherings. We install chandeliers at proper heights above dining tables, typically 30 to 36 inches above table surfaces. This positioning provides good illumination without blocking sight lines across tables.

Chandelier installations require secure mounting to support fixture weight. Large chandeliers may require ceiling reinforcement beyond standard electrical boxes. We ensure mounting systems safely support chandelier weight and movement. Dimmer switches allow you to adjust chandelier brightness for different occasions from bright family dinners to intimate gatherings.

Outdoor Lighting Installation Services

Outdoor lighting extends your living space into evenings, improves security, and enhances your home's curb appeal. We provide complete outdoor lighting services designed for Las Vegas climate conditions.

Security Lighting Installation

Security lighting deters crime and improves safety around your home. We install motion-sensor lights at entry points, garages, and dark areas where intruders might approach. Motion sensors activate lights automatically when movement is detected, startling intruders and alerting you to activity around your home.

Security lights should illuminate areas thoroughly without creating glare that blinds you or neighbors. We position lights carefully to maximize coverage while minimizing light pollution. LED security lights provide bright illumination using minimal electricity, important for lights that may run for hours each night.

Photocell controls automatically turn outdoor lights on at dusk and off at dawn. This automatic operation ensures security lighting operates every night without requiring you to remember switches. Combined with motion sensors, photocells provide efficient, automatic security lighting protection.

Pathway and Walkway Lighting

Pathway lighting improves safety by illuminating walkways, steps, and potential tripping hazards. We install low-voltage pathway lights along walks, driveways, and steps. These lights guide visitors safely to entry points while creating attractive nighttime landscapes.

Pathway lights should provide adequate illumination without excessive brightness. Properly designed pathway lighting creates inviting approaches to your home while maintaining comfortable light levels. LED pathway lights last for years with minimal maintenance, important for lights installed in landscaping.

Porch and Patio Lighting

Covered porches and patios benefit from lighting that extends usable hours into evenings. We install ceiling lights, wall sconces, and recessed lights on covered outdoor living areas. All outdoor fixtures are rated for damp or wet locations depending on exposure levels. All outdoor lighting includes proper GFCI protection as required by code.

Patio lighting creates comfortable outdoor rooms for evening relaxation and entertaining. Multiple light sources at different heights create layered lighting that's both functional and attractive. Dimmer controls allow you to adjust outdoor lighting for different activities and moods.

Landscape Lighting Design

Landscape lighting highlights architectural features, illuminates trees and plants, and creates beautiful nighttime views. We design landscape lighting systems that showcase your property's best features while providing subtle illumination throughout outdoor spaces.

Uplighting illuminates trees, architectural details, and focal points from below. This dramatic lighting creates depth and interest in nighttime landscapes. Downlighting from trees provides gentle ambient lighting that mimics natural moonlight. Path lights guide movement through yards while spotlights highlight specimen plants or water features.

All landscape lighting installations include proper waterproofing and burial depths for wiring. We use quality fixtures designed for long-term outdoor exposure in Las Vegas climate conditions. Professional landscape lighting systems operate reliably for years with minimal maintenance.

Recessed Lighting Installation

Recessed lighting, also called can lights or downlights, provides clean, modern illumination throughout homes. These fixtures mount into ceilings with only the trim visible from below. We install recessed lighting in new construction and retrofit installations in existing homes. Installations may require additional electrical wiring for new circuits.

Planning Recessed Lighting Layouts

Proper recessed light spacing ensures even illumination without dark spots or overlighting. We design layouts based on room dimensions, ceiling heights, and lighting purposes. General illumination requires lights spaced appropriately to overlap their light patterns. Task lighting uses closer spacing for brighter, more focused illumination.

Recessed lighting works well in rooms with low ceilings where hanging fixtures would hang too low. The flush installation maintains maximum ceiling height while providing excellent illumination. Kitchens, bathrooms, hallways, and living areas all benefit from recessed lighting.

Types of Recessed Fixtures

Recessed lights come in various sizes and types. Standard sizes include 4-inch, 5-inch, and 6-inch diameter fixtures. Larger fixtures provide more light and work better for general illumination in large rooms. Smaller fixtures suit task lighting and spaces where less light is needed.

IC-rated recessed lights can contact insulation safely. Non-IC lights require clearance from insulation. We install appropriate fixture types for your ceiling construction and insulation. Airtight fixtures reduce energy loss through ceiling penetrations, important in climate-controlled Las Vegas homes.

LED Retrofit and Upgrades

Older recessed lights with incandescent or halogen bulbs waste energy and generate excessive heat. LED retrofit kits convert existing recessed lights to energy-efficient LED technology. These retrofits reduce electricity consumption by 75% or more while providing better light quality.

We install LED recessed lights in new installations and retrofit existing fixtures throughout homes. LED lights last 15 to 25 years with normal use, virtually eliminating bulb replacement for decades. The combination of energy savings and long life makes LED lighting highly cost-effective.

Specialty Lighting Installation

Beyond standard lighting fixtures, we install specialty lighting that serves specific purposes or creates particular effects throughout your homes.

Under-Cabinet Lighting

Under-cabinet lighting eliminates shadows on countertops, making kitchen work safer and easier. We install LED strip lights or puck lights under cabinets to provide focused task lighting exactly where you need it. These lights plug into outlets or connect directly to electrical circuits for permanent installation.

Modern under-cabinet lights use LED technology that produces minimal heat, important for installations near cabinets and walls. Dimming controls allow you to adjust brightness from full task lighting to soft night-light levels.

Closet Lighting

Closet lighting helps you find clothes and see colors accurately. We install ceiling fixtures or LED strip lighting in closets of all sizes. Motion sensors automatically activate closet lights when doors open, providing convenient hands-free operation. Lights turn off automatically when doors close, saving energy.

Building codes specify minimum distances between light fixtures and stored items in closets. We ensure all closet lighting installations meet safety requirements for clearances and fixture types.

Accent and Display Lighting

Accent lighting highlights artwork, collections, or architectural features. We install track lighting, picture lights, and spotlights that draw attention to items you want to showcase. Proper accent lighting creates dramatic effects while preventing damage to sensitive items from excessive light or heat.

Display cabinet lighting illuminates collectibles and valuables. We install LED lighting in display cabinets that provides excellent color rendering without harmful UV radiation or excessive heat. These installations enhance the beauty of displayed items while protecting them.

Lighting Control Systems

Modern lighting controls provide convenience and energy savings. We install various control systems from simple dimmers to sophisticated smart home integration.

Dimmer Switch Installation

Dimmer switches allow you to adjust light levels for different activities and times of day. Dimming lights saves energy and extends bulb life. We install dimmers compatible with LED lights, which require specific dimmer types for proper operation.

Three-way dimming systems allow light control from multiple locations. These installations work well in large rooms with multiple entry points or in stairways where you want control from both levels.

Smart Lighting Controls

Smart switches and bulbs integrate with home automation systems for voice control and smartphone operation. We install smart lighting controls that work with popular systems including Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Apple HomeKit.

Smart lighting allows you to create schedules, control lights remotely, and integrate lighting with other home automation functions. You can turn lights on before arriving home, create vacation lighting schedules, and adjust lighting without getting up.

Motion Sensor Controls

Motion sensors automatically turn lights on when you enter rooms and off when rooms are vacant. These controls save energy in rooms where people forget to turn off lights. We install motion sensors in bathrooms, garages, laundry rooms, and other utility spaces where automatic control provides convenience and efficiency.

LED Lighting Upgrades

LED technology has revolutionized home lighting by providing excellent light quality with minimal energy consumption. We help Las Vegas homeowners upgrade to LED lighting throughout their homes.

Benefits of LED Lighting

LED lights use 75% less electricity than incandescent bulbs while producing the same light output. This dramatic energy savings reduces cooling costs because LEDs also generate much less heat. In Las Vegas where air conditioning runs for months, reducing heat from lighting provides significant additional savings.

LED bulbs last 15 to 25 years with typical use. This longevity eliminates frequent bulb replacement and reduces maintenance needs. The combination of energy savings and long life makes LED lighting extremely cost-effective despite higher initial costs.

Converting to LED Lighting

Converting existing fixtures to LED lighting often requires only changing bulbs. However, some fixtures need modifications for optimal LED performance. We help you identify which fixtures need simple bulb changes and which benefit from fixture upgrades or replacements.

Enclosed fixtures trap heat that can shorten LED life. We recommend LED bulbs rated for enclosed fixtures in these locations. Outdoor fixtures need LED bulbs rated for temperature extremes common in Las Vegas. Using appropriate LED bulbs ensures long life and reliable performance.

Electrical Considerations for Lighting

Proper lighting installation requires adequate electrical infrastructure. We ensure your electrical system can support new lighting safely and reliably.

Circuit Capacity for Lighting

New lighting installations must connect to circuits with adequate capacity. We calculate total load on circuits before adding lights. Overloaded circuits trip breakers and create safety hazards. When necessary, we install dedicated circuits for new lighting.

Large lighting projects may require electrical panel upgrades to provide adequate circuits and capacity. We assess your electrical system and recommend appropriate upgrades to support your lighting plans.

Wiring for New Lighting

Installing lights where none existed requires running new electrical wiring. We fish wiring through walls and ceilings, minimizing damage to finished surfaces. Proper wiring installation includes appropriate wire sizes, proper connections, and code-compliant routing.

All electrical work includes proper permits and inspections as required by local building departments. We handle all permitting and ensure installations pass inspection.
