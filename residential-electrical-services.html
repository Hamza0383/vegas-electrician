Residential Electrical Services We Offer

Electrical Panel Upgrades

Your panel runs everything in your home. When it cannot keep up, problems start.

A 1990s home in Henderson or Summerlin usually has a 100-amp panel. Back then that was fine. Today that same home has two AC units, a pool pump, and an EV charger. The old panel cannot handle it.

We check your actual load before we do anything. If you need a 200-amp upgrade, we do it. We replace old Federal Pacific and Zinsco panels. We install subpanels for pools, workshops, and garages.

Every panel job is pulled with a Clark County permit and inspected before we close it out.

Home Wiring and Rewiring

This is one of the most important jobs we do. It is also one of the most overlooked.

Homes built before 1980 in areas like Paradise, Winchester, and Downtown Las Vegas often have aluminum branch wiring. It looks fine from the outside. But aluminum moves more than copper does. Over time that loosens connections. Loose connections get hot. Hot connections start fires.

Some older valley homes still have knob-and-tube wiring. If your home has it, your insurance may not cover a fire caused by it.

We rewire partial circuits and full homes. We pull the permits. We get the work inspected. We do not cut corners.

Circuit Breaker Repair and Replacement

Not every breaker problem means you need a new panel. Sometimes one bad breaker is the issue.

A breaker that trips every day, will not reset, feels hot, or shows scorch marks needs to come out now. We test the circuit first to make sure the breaker is the problem and not the wiring behind it.

We carry common breaker brands on the truck. Most replacements are done the same day.

Outlet and Switch Repairs

Dead outlets are one of the most common calls we get. Most of the time a GFCI has tripped on a different circuit. But sometimes the outlet has burned out inside — especially in older homes.

We replace two-prong outlets with grounded three-prong outlets. We install USB outlets in kitchens and offices. We replace dimmers that buzz, flicker, or run warm.

If an outlet feels warm when nothing is plugged in, that is a wiring problem. Call us today.

Lighting Installation

We install recessed lighting in living rooms, kitchens, and hallways. We run circuits for under-cabinet kitchen lighting. We put up exterior wall lights, soffit lighting, and security lights.

Outdoor lighting in Las Vegas has to hold up to 115-degree heat, UV, and monsoon rains. We use fixtures rated for all of it. Cheap outdoor fixtures do not last one summer here.

Ceiling Fan Installation and Repair

A ceiling fan lets you set the thermostat a few degrees higher. Over a full Las Vegas summer that saves real money on your NV Energy bill.

We install fans in bedrooms, living rooms, patios, and covered outdoor areas. We swap out standard outlet boxes for fan-rated boxes. A regular box is not strong enough to hold a moving fan safely.

We also fix fans that wobble, hum, or have flickering light kits. Most fan repairs are quick — a loose blade or a bad capacitor.

GFCI and AFCI Protection

GFCI outlets stop shocks near water. AFCI breakers stop arc faults that cause fires. Both are required by code in different parts of your home.

Many Las Vegas homes built before 2000 have neither. We update kitchens, bathrooms, garages, and outdoor areas with proper GFCI protection. We install AFCI breakers where code requires them.

Outdoor outlet dead after monsoon rains? The GFCI tripped or the housing cracked. We replace it the same visit.

EV Charger Installation

A Level 2 charger needs its own 240-volt, 50-amp circuit. Before we run any wire, we check what your panel can still handle. If you are close to the limit, we tell you before we start.

We pull the permit, run the circuit, mount the charger, and test it. Most installs are done in one day.

Electrical Safety Inspections

Buying an older home in Henderson, Paradise, or Spring Valley? Get the electrical checked before you close.

We open the panel and look for loose connections, corroded parts, and signs of heat damage. We test every outlet we can reach. We check GFCI and AFCI protection. We look at the service entry and outdoor conduit.

We give you a straight report on what we find. No pressure. No upselling.

Room by Room – Where Problems Hide in Las Vegas Homes

Kitchen – High-draw appliances on shared circuits. Outlets with no GFCI protection. Under-cabinet lighting on already loaded circuits.

Bathrooms – No GFCI near water. Old exhaust fans with no dedicated circuit. Dimmers on lights that are not dimmer rated.

Garage – No GFCI on outlets. No dedicated circuit for a fridge or tools. Old wiring that cannot handle an EV charger.

Outdoor Areas – Fixtures not rated for heat and moisture. Cracked conduit from years of heat cycles. GFCI outlets that have never been tested.

Bedrooms – No AFCI protection. Overloaded circuits from TVs and gaming setups. Fans mounted on boxes that were not made for them.

Frequently Asked Questions

My outlet works sometimes and not others. What causes that?

A loose connection inside the outlet or the box behind it. Left alone, loose connections heat up and become a fire risk. Call us and we will find it.

Can I add a hot tub to my existing electrical system?

Most hot tubs need a dedicated 240-volt, 50-amp circuit with a nearby disconnect box. Whether your panel can handle it depends on your current load. We check before we run any wire.

Why does my kitchen outlet not work but my bathroom outlet does?

They are likely on the same GFCI circuit. The reset button may be in the bathroom, not the kitchen. If resetting it does not help, the circuit has a fault that needs to be traced.

How do I know if my home has aluminum wiring?

Look at any visible wire in your attic or panel. Aluminum wiring is marked AL. Not sure? We check it on any service call at no extra charge.
