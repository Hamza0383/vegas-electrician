Electrical Panel Upgrade Services

Your electrical panel distributes power throughout your entire property. An outdated or overloaded panel creates serious safety hazards and prevents you from adding new electrical devices. Most Las Vegas homes built before 2000 need electrical panel upgrades to meet modern electrical demands.

Signs You Need a Panel Upgrade

Signs you need a panel upgrade include breakers that trip frequently, lights that dim when you run certain appliances, a panel that feels warm to the touch, or inability to add new circuits for appliances or EV chargers. If your home has a 100-amp panel, upgrading to 200-amp service provides the capacity needed for today's electrical requirements.

Panel Upgrade Process and Timeline

Our electrical panel upgrade process starts with a thorough inspection of your current system. We calculate your electrical load requirements, check for code violations, and provide upfront pricing. We handle all permits and coordinate with the utility company. Learn more about our electrical panel upgrade process. Installation typically takes one day, and we ensure your power is only off intermittently during the upgrade process.

After Installation

After installation, a city inspector reviews our work to verify code compliance. We repair any exterior damage, label all breakers clearly, and walk you through your new panel. You receive complete documentation including permits, inspection reports, and warranty information. The result is a modern, safe electrical system that handles today's demands and provides capacity for future needs.

Electrical Wiring and Rewiring Services

Faulty wiring represents one of the most serious electrical hazards in Las Vegas homes. The dry desert climate causes wire insulation to crack and deteriorate over time. Old wiring that worked fine for decades can suddenly become dangerous as insulation breaks down, exposing live wires.

Problems with Outdated Wiring

Homes built before 1980 often contain outdated wiring that doesn't meet current safety standards. Aluminum wiring, common in homes from the 1960s and 1970s, poses particular risks. Knob-and-tube wiring found in very old homes cannot safely handle modern electrical loads. DIY electrical work or repairs by unlicensed contractors often create hidden dangers behind your walls.

Complete Rewiring Services

Our rewiring services address these problems completely. We identify faulty wiring, plan the rewiring project to minimize disruption, and replace old wiring with modern, properly rated materials. The work includes updating outlets to three-prong grounded receptacles, installing proper circuit protection, and ensuring all connections meet current electrical codes.

Benefits of Home Rewiring

Rewiring protects your family and property from electrical fires. It also increases your home's value and makes future electrical additions easier. While rewiring represents a significant investment, it provides decades of safe, reliable electrical service. We work efficiently to complete the project quickly while maintaining the highest quality standards.

GFCI Outlet Installation for Safety

Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter outlets protect you from dangerous electrical shocks in areas where water and electricity might meet. Building codes require GFCI outlets in bathrooms, kitchens, garages, outdoor areas, and near pools or spas. If your Las Vegas home lacks these safety devices, you're at risk.

GFCI outlets detect electrical imbalances and shut off power in milliseconds when they sense danger. This protection is especially important in Las Vegas homes with pools, where the combination of water and electricity creates serious hazards. Older homes often have standard outlets in locations where GFCI protection is now required.

Our electricians install GFCI outlets wherever they're needed for safety or code compliance. We also test existing GFCI outlets to ensure they function properly. A GFCI outlet that doesn't trip when tested provides no protection and should be replaced immediately.

Pool owners throughout Henderson, Summerlin, and other Las Vegas neighborhoods trust us to install proper GFCI protection around their pools and spas. This protection isn't optional—it's essential for preventing electrical accidents that can cause serious injury or death.

Circuit Breaker Repair and Replacement

Circuit breakers protect your electrical system by shutting off power when circuits become overloaded. When breakers trip frequently, fail to reset, or don't trip when they should, they need professional attention. Old or defective breakers create serious fire hazards.

Breakers trip for good reasons—they're preventing dangerous overloads. If your breakers trip constantly, the problem might be overloaded circuits, short circuits, ground faults, or the breakers themselves. Our electricians diagnose the cause and recommend appropriate solutions.

Sometimes circuit breaker problems indicate that your electrical panel needs upgrading. A 100-amp panel with overloaded circuits trips breakers frequently because it simply cannot handle your electrical demands. In these cases, panel replacement solves the problem permanently.

We replace defective circuit breakers with proper replacements that match your panel specifications. All breaker replacements meet current electrical codes and pass inspection. After service, your circuits operate reliably without nuisance tripping.

Smoke Detector Installation and Maintenance

Working smoke detectors save lives by providing early warning of fires. Las Vegas building codes require smoke detectors in specific locations throughout homes. Older homes often lack adequate smoke detector coverage or have outdated detectors that should be replaced.

We install hardwired smoke detectors with battery backup for maximum reliability. Hardwired detectors receive constant power while battery backup ensures they work during power outages. Modern smoke detectors also include interconnection, so when one alarm sounds, all alarms throughout your home activate.

Smoke detector placement matters. Building codes specify locations based on home layout and size. We install detectors in bedrooms, hallways, and common areas according to current requirements. Proper installation ensures detectors can sense smoke quickly and alert you to danger.

Smoke detectors have limited lifespans. Detectors older than ten years should be replaced regardless of whether they still function. We test existing detectors and replace any that are outdated or non-functional. This simple service provides essential protection for your family.
