Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Vegas Electrician ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website vegaselectricianservice.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact forms. When you fill out a contact or quote request form, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and any details you provide about your electrical project.
  • Phone calls. When you call us at (702) 899-7099, we may collect your name and phone number as part of normal business communication.
  • Automatic data. When you visit our website, our hosting and analytics providers may automatically collect your IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL, and the date and time of your visit.
  • Cookies. Our site may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience and gather usage data. See Section 5 for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your service inquiries and schedule appointments
  • Provide electrical services and follow up on completed work
  • Send you quotes, invoices, or other information related to your request
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors use it
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing without your consent.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers. Third-party vendors who help us operate our website or run our business (e.g., web hosting, analytics, scheduling software). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may only use it to perform services on our behalf.
  • Legal requirements. If required by law, court order, or government authority, we may disclose your information.
  • Business transfers. If Vegas Electrician is acquired or merges with another business, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Contact and job records are typically kept for a period consistent with standard business and tax recordkeeping requirements.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to remember your preferences and analyze traffic. We may use third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics, which collects anonymous usage data. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some functionality of our site.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as Google Maps or review platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you provide from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt out of any future communications from us

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
