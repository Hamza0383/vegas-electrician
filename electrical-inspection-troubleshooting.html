What an Inspection in Las Vegas Covers

Panel & Breaker Condition

We check the panel inside and out. Heat, corrosion, and damage all noted. Breakers tested. Federal Pacific and Zinsco are common in older homes here. Both have known failure issues.

Wiring Safety & Code Check

We check the wiring throughout the home. Aluminum wiring, old insulation, open boxes, loose wires — all noted. Anything not meeting code gets flagged.

Outlet, GFCI & AFCI Check

Every outlet we can reach gets tested. GFCI protection confirmed in all required spots. Kitchens, bathrooms, garages, and outdoor areas. We check AFCI where code requires it. Missing GFCI or AFCI is one of the most common issues found in local homes.

Grounding & Surge Protection

We check the grounding and confirm connections at the panel. Surge protection reviewed. Summer storms here can spike the grid fast.

Troubleshooting

Electrical Troubleshooting in Las Vegas

01

Diagnosing Intermittent Power Loss

Power that cuts out and comes back on its own is hard to trace. It could be a loose joint at the panel. It could be a bad wire inside a wall. Diagnostic tools track it down. No guessing. Just testing.

02

Tracking Down Tripping Breakers & Wiring Faults

A breaker that trips on the same circuit again and again is pointing to something real. Heavy load. Short circuit. Ground fault. Bad device on the line. Every electrical inspection Las Vegas troubleshooting call starts with a proper diagnosis. Not just swapping parts.

03

Finding the Source of Odors or Sparks

A burning smell or spark from an outlet is a warning. Source located fast. Outlet, wiring behind it, and the circuit all checked. Cause found and fixed.
